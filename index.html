Welcome! I am a postdoctoral researcher Massachusetts General Hospital and MIT, and I recently received my PhD in Systems Biology from Harvard University. This page exists primarily to host details about my academic interests.

Mouse suprachiasmatic nucleus (image: C. Mazuski, Herzog lab, WUSTL)

In general, my research lies at the interface of systems biology, neuroscience, complex dynamical systems, and control theory. You can read about my research interest here. My doctoral research was focused on the mammalian circadian oscillator and the relationship between its biological function and its mathematical structure.
