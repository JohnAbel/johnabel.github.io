Circadian Rhythms - Control

Abel JH, Chakrabarty A, Klerman EB, Doyle III FJ. Pharmaceutical-based entrainment of circadian phase via nonlinear model predictive control, Automatica 100, 2019.
We show that model predictive control for circadian resetting can follow the optimal control law except for regions where the PRC crosses 0. We use this to prove bounds on how far from optimality a controller will be as a function of timestep size, and implicitly, pharmacokinetics.

Abel JH, Chakrabarty A, Doyle III FJ. Controlling biological time: nonlinear model predictive control for circadian oscillator populations. Emerging Applications of Control and System Theory, R Tempo, S Yurkovich, P Misra Eds. New York, NY: Springer Publishing, 2018. ISBN: 978-3-319-67068-3
We show that control of circadian oscillation may synchronize or desynchronize a population of cells, and provide a simple MPC formulation that is designed to achieve synchrony.

Abel JH, Chakrabarty A, Doyle III FJ. Nonlinear model predictive control for circadian entrainment using small-molecule pharmaceuticals. Proceedings of the 20th IFAC World Congress, 2017.
We demonstrate by extensive simulations that the model predictive control performance depends on prediction horizon and step size for circadian resetting.

Abel JH, Doyle III FJ. A systems theoretic approach to analysis and control of mammalian circadian dynamics. Chemical Engineering Research and Design. 116, 2016.
We review sensitivity analyses of limit cycle oscillators and use these metrics to develop a nonlinear model predictive control scheme for the circadian clock.

Jung S, Abel JH, Starger J, and Yi H. Porosity-tuned chitosan-polyacrylamide hydrogel microspheres for improved protein conjugation. Biomacromolecules 17 (7), 2016.
We present a method for synthesizing hydrogel microspheres from polyethylene glycol diacrylate and chitosan, and show that we can induce macroporosity to increase capacity for biofunctionalization.
